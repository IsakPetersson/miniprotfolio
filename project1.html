Webshop

This feature allows users to add products to a shopping cart and proceed to checkout and receive a receipt.

Project Management

A project management feature to help users add, remove and customise the product listings.

The webshop includes a store page with multiple products, a shopping cart that updates in real-time with the possibility to change amounts and remove products, a checkout where users can download a receipt in txt format and a form for user feedback.

The project management section includes a login and authentication system, the ability to add, remove and edit products, and a dashboard to view all products in the database. But also a way for users to log in.

The project was hosted on a raspberry pi, storing the database of products and users.
